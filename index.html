See what's behind social links with HoverCards

HoverCards is a chrome extension that lets you see what's behind links from youtube, twitter, reddit, soundcloud, imgur, & instagram all with out ever leaving the web page you're currently on.

Add HoverCards to Chrome

Open fewer tabs, click a hovercard for more

Clicking a hovercard not only shows you what's behind the link; it shows you what impact it has across the web. For example, click an imgur hovercard & see what redditors are saying about the image. Or click a twitter account hovercard & see their instagram and youtube accounts.
